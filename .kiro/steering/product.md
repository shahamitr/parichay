# Product Overview

**Parichay** is a multi-brand microsite builder platform for creating SEO-friendly digital business card microsites.

## Core Purpose
Enable businesses to create and manage dynamic microsites with a no-code builder, supporting multi-brand and multi-branch management with subscription-based monetization.

## Key Features
- **Dynamic URL-based microsites**: Pattern `/{brand}/{branch}` for SEO-friendly URLs
- **Multi-brand & multi-branch management**: Centralized dashboard for multiple brands and locations
- **No-code builder**: Drag-and-drop interface with dynamic theming
- **White-label support**: Multi-tenant architecture for agencies
- **Subscription management**: Tiered plans with automated billing and licensing
- **Lead management**: Capture, route, and track leads with CRM integration
- **Analytics**: Comprehensive metrics and reporting
- **QR code generation**: Downloadable QR codes with analytics tracking
- **Payment processing**: Integrated with Stripe and Razorpay

## User Roles
- **SUPER_ADMIN**: Platform administrator
- **BRAND_MANAGER**: Manages brands and subscriptions
- **BRANCH_ADMIN**: Manages individual branch microsites
- **EXECUTIVE**: Onboards branches and manages client relationships

## Business Model
Subscription-based SaaS with tiered plans (monthly/yearly), payment gateway integration, and automated invoicing.
