# OneTouch BizCard - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is OneTouch BizCard?

OneTouch BizCard is a platform that helps businesses create professional digital business cards and microsites. Each microsite is a single-page website that showcases your business information, services, and contact details. Visitors can easily contact you with one touch using QR codes or direct links.

### Who is OneTouch BizCard for?

OneTouch BizCard is perfect for:
- Small businesses with multiple locations
- Franchises and chain stores
- Real estate agents
- Sales professionals
- Service providers
- Restaurants and cafes
- Retail stores
- Any business that wants a professional online presence

### How is this different from a regular website?

OneTouch BizCard microsites are:
- **Faster to create**: Set up in minutes, not days
- **Mobile-optimized**: Designed for smartphone viewing
- **QR code enabled**: Easy to share with physical materials
- **Location-specific**: Create unique pages for each branch
- **Lead-focused**: Built to capture customer information
- **No coding required**: Easy drag-and-drop editor

## Brands and Branches

### What's the difference between a brand and a branch?

- **Brand**: Your company or organization (e.g., "Acme Corporation")
- **Branch**: A specific location or division (e.g., "Downtown Store", "North Office")

Each brand can have multiple branches, and each branch gets its own microsite.

### How many branches can I create?

Depends on your plan:
- **Free**: 1 branch
- **Starter**: 3 branches
- **Professional**: 10 branches
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited branches

### Can I have multiple brands?

Yes! You can create multiple brands under one account. Each brand can have its own logo, colors, and branches.

### Can I delete a branch?

Yes, you can delete branches at any time. Note that this will permanently remove the microsite and all associated data.

### What happens to my branches if I downgrade?

If you downgrade to a plan with fewer branches, you'll need to deactivate branches to meet the limit. Deactivated branches are preserved but not publicly accessible.

## Microsites

### What is a microsite?

A microsite is a single-page website for your branch. It includes:
- Business information
- Contact details
- Services or products
- Photo gallery
- Contact form
- Social media links

### How do I customize my microsite?

1. Go to Dashboard → Branches
2. Select a branch
3. Click "Edit Microsite"
4. Use the drag-and-drop editor to customize sections
5. Click "Publish" to make changes live

### Can I use my own domain?

Yes, with Professional or Enterprise plans. You can use:
- Subdomain: cards.yourcompany.com
- Custom domain: yourcompany.com

### How do I change templates?

### Can I have different templates for different branches?

Yes! Each branch can use a different template.

### Are microsites mobile-friendly?

Yes! All microsites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices.

### Can I add custom code?

Custom code is available with Enterprise plans. Contact us for details.

## QR Codes

### What is a QR code?

A QR code is a scannable barcode that links directly to your microsite. When someone scans it with their phone camera, they're taken to your digital business card.

### How do I create a QR code?

1. Go to your branch page
2. Click "QR Codes" tab
3. Click "+ Generate QR Code"
4. Choose size and format
5. Click "Download"

### What formats are available?

- **PNG**: For digital use (websites, emails, social media)
- **SVG**: For print (business cards, flyers, posters)

### Can I customize my QR code?

Yes, with paid plans you can:
- Add your logo in the center
- Change colors to match your brand
- Add a frame with text

### Where should I use QR codes?

- Business cards
- Flyers and brochures
- Posters
- Product packaging
- Store windows
- Email signatures
- Social media posts
- Presentations

### Do QR codes expire?

No! QR codes are permanent and work as long as your account is active.

### Can I track QR code scans?

Yes! View analytics for:
- Total scans
- Scans by date
- Scans by location
- Device types

## Lead Management

### What is a lead?

A lead is a potential customer who filled out your contact form. Leads include:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Message
- Source (which microsite)
- Date and time

### How do I receive lead notifications?

Leads are sent to:
- **Email**: Branch email address
- **WhatsApp**: Branch WhatsApp number (if configured)
- **Dashboard**: In-app notification

### Can I export leads?

Yes! Export leads as CSV files for use in:
- Excel or Google Sheets
- CRM systems
- Email marketing tools

### How do I manage leads?

1. Go to Dashboard → Leads
2. View all leads
3. Mark as:
   - Contacted
   - Converted
   - Not interested
4. Add notes to each lead

### Can I integrate with my CRM?

Yes, Enterprise plans include CRM integration for:
- Salesforce
- HubSpot
- Zoho CRM
- Custom integrations via API

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

Try these steps:
1. Check your email and password
2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if still having issues

### My microsite isn't loading

Check:
1. Is your branch active?
2. Is the URL correct?
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Try a different browser or device
5. Check your internet connection

### My QR code isn't working

Try:
1. Test with a different QR code scanner app
2. Ensure the QR code is large enough (at least 2cm x 2cm)
3. Check for good lighting when scanning
4. Regenerate the QR code
5. Verify the URL is correct

### Images aren't uploading

Check:
1. File size (max 5MB)
2. File format (PNG, JPG, SVG only)
3. Internet connection
4. Try compressing the image
5. Try a different browser

### Payment failed

Try:
1. Verify card details are correct
2. Check card has sufficient funds
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your bank
5. Contact our support team
