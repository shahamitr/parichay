## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Website**
   - Go to https://zintro.com
   - Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner

2. **Fill in Your Details**
   - Enter your full name
   - Provide your email address
   - Create a strong password
   - Click "Create Account"

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link
   - You'll be redirected to the login page

4. **First Login**
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"
   - You'll be taken to the onboarding wizard

### Onboarding Wizard

The onboarding wizard helps you set up your first brand and branch:

**Step 1: Brand Information**
- Enter your brand name
- Upload your logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
- Choose your brand colors
- Add a brief description

**Step 2: First Branch**
- Enter branch name (e.g., "Main Office", "Downtown Store")
- Add contact information:
  - Phone number
  - Email address
  - Physical address
- Set business hours

**Step 3: Choose a Template**
- Browse available templates
- Preview templates
- Select the one that fits your business
- Click "Complete Setup"

Congratulations! Your first microsite is now live!

## Brand Management

### Creating a New Brand

1. **Navigate to Brands**
   - Click "Brands" in the sidebar
   - Click "+ New Brand" button

2. **Enter Brand Details**
   - **Brand Name**: Your company or brand name
   - **Slug**: URL-friendly name (auto-generated, can be customized)
   - **Logo**: Upload your brand logo
     - Recommended size: 500x500px
     - Formats: PNG, JPG, SVG
     - Max size: 5MB
   - **Description**: Brief description of your brand

3. **Brand Theme**
   - **Primary Color**: Main brand color
   - **Secondary Color**: Accent color
   - **Font**: Choose from available fonts
   - Preview your theme in real-time

4. **Save Brand**
   - Click "Create Brand"
   - Your brand is now created!

### Editing Brand Information

1. Go to "Brands" in the sidebar
2. Click on the brand you want to edit
3. Click "Edit Brand" button
4. Make your changes
5. Click "Save Changes"

### Brand Settings

**Custom Domain** (Premium feature)
- Add your own domain (e.g., cards.yourcompany.com)
- Follow DNS configuration instructions
- Verify domain ownership
- SSL certificate is automatically provisioned

**Brand Visibility**
- Public: Anyone can view your microsites
- Private: Only accessible with direct link

## Branch Management

### Creating a Branch

1. **Select Your Brand**
   - Click "Branches" in the sidebar
   - Select the brand from dropdown

2. **Add Branch Details**
   - **Branch Name**: e.g., "Downtown Store", "North Branch"
   - **Slug**: URL-friendly name
   - **Contact Information**:
     - Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
     - Email: branch@example.com
     - WhatsApp: +1 (555) 123-4567
   - **Address**:
     - Street address
     - City
     - State/Province
     - ZIP/Postal code
     - Country

3. **Business Hours**
   - Set hours for each day of the week
   - Mark closed days
   - Add special hours (holidays, etc.)

4. **Social Media Links**
   - Facebook
   - Instagram
   - Twitter/X
   - LinkedIn
   - YouTube

5. **Save Branch**
   - Click "Create Branch"
   - Your branch microsite is now live!

### Branch URL

Your branch microsite will be accessible at:
```no-highlight
https://zintro.com/[brand-slug]/[branch-slug]
```

Example:
```no-highlight
https://zintro.com/acme-corp/downtown-store
```

### Managing Multiple Branches

- View all branches in a list
- Filter by brand
- Search by name
- Sort by creation date or name
- Bulk actions (coming soon)

## Microsite Builder

### Accessing the Builder

1. Go to "Branches"
2. Click on a branch
3. Click "Edit Microsite" button

### Microsite Sections

Your microsite is made up of customizable sections:

#### 1. Hero Section
- **Headline**: Main message (e.g., "Welcome to Our Store")
- **Subheadline**: Supporting text
- **Background Image**: Upload a hero image
- **Call-to-Action Button**: Text and link

#### 2. About Section
- **Title**: Section heading
- **Content**: Rich text editor
  - Add formatted text
  - Insert images
  - Create lists
  - Add links
- **Image**: Optional side image

#### 3. Services Section
- **Title**: "Our Services" or custom
- **Services List**:
  - Service name
  - Description
  - Icon (choose from library)
  - Price (optional)
- Add/remove services as needed

#### 4. Gallery Section
- **Title**: "Gallery" or custom
- **Images**: Upload multiple images
  - Drag and drop to reorder
  - Click to enlarge
  - Add captions
- **Layout**: Grid or carousel

#### 5. Contact Section
- **Title**: "Get in Touch" or custom
- **Contact Buttons**:
  - Call: Direct phone call
  - WhatsApp: Open WhatsApp chat
  - Email: Send email
  - Directions: Open in maps
- **Lead Capture Form**:
  - Name field
  - Email field
  - Phone field
  - Message field
  - Custom fields (add your own)

#### ✨ AI Content Assistance (New!)
- **Generate with AI**: Next to text fields like headlines, descriptions, and content areas, you'll find a "✨ Generate with AI" button.
- **How it works**:
  1. Click the "✨ Generate with AI" button.
  2. A popup will appear. Enter a few keywords about your business (e.g., "eco-friendly dry cleaner in Brooklyn").
  3. Select a desired tone (e.g., Professional, Casual, Witty).
  4. Click "Generate".
  5. The AI will provide several content suggestions.
  6. Click "Use this" on your favorite suggestion to instantly populate the field.
  7. You can then edit the text as needed.
- **Benefits**:
  - Overcome writer's block.
  - Create professional copy in seconds.
  - Ensure your messaging is compelling and on-brand.

### Drag-and-Drop Section Management

1. **Reorder Sections**
   - Click and hold the drag handle (⋮⋮)
   - Drag section up or down
   - Release to drop in new position

2. **Enable/Disable Sections**
   - Toggle switch next to each section
   - Disabled sections won't appear on your microsite
   - Content is preserved when disabled

3. **Edit Section Content**
   - Click "Edit" button on any section
   - Make your changes in the editor
   - Click "Save" to apply changes

### Undo/Redo

- **Undo**: Ctrl+Z (Windows) or Cmd+Z (Mac)
- **Redo**: Ctrl+Y (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Z (Mac)
- Undo/Redo buttons in the toolbar
- Up to 50 actions can be undone

### Template Selection

1. Click "Change Template" button
2. Browse available templates
3. Preview each template
4. Click "Apply Template"
5. Your content is automatically adapted to the new template

### Preview Modes

Test your microsite on different devices:
- **Desktop**: Full-width view
- **Tablet**: iPad-sized view
- **Mobile**: Phone-sized view

Click the device icons in the toolbar to switch views.

### Publishing Changes

1. Make your edits
2. Click "Save Draft" to save without publishing
3. Click "Publish" to make changes live
4. Changes are live immediately

## QR Code Generation

### Creating a QR Code

1. **Navigate to QR Codes**
   - Go to "Branches"
   - Select a branch
   - Click "QR Codes" tab

2. **Generate QR Code**
   - Click "+ Generate QR Code" button
   - Choose format:
     - PNG (for digital use)
     - SVG (for print, scalable)
   - Choose size:
     - Small (256x256)
     - Medium (512x512)
     - Large (1024x1024)
   - Click "Generate"

3. **Customize QR Code** (Premium feature)
   - Add logo in center
   - Change colors to match brand
   - Add frame with text

### Downloading QR Codes

1. Click "Download" button
2. Choose format (PNG or SVG)
3. File downloads automatically

### Using QR Codes

**Print Materials**:
- Business cards
- Flyers and brochures
- Posters
- Product packaging
- Store windows

**Digital Use**:
- Email signatures
- Social media posts
- Website
- Digital presentations

### QR Code Analytics

View scan statistics:
- Total scans
- Scans by date
- Scans by location (country, city)
- Device types
- Recent scan activity

## Analytics Dashboard

### Accessing Analytics

1. Click "Analytics" in the sidebar
2. Select brand and branch
3. Choose date range

### Key Metrics

**Overview Cards**:
- **Page Views**: Total visits to your microsite
- **Unique Visitors**: Number of unique people
- **QR Scans**: Total QR code scans
- **Leads Captured**: Form submissions

### Charts and Graphs

**Activity Trend**:
- Line chart showing activity over time
- View by day, week, or month
- Compare different metrics

**Click Breakdown**:
- Pie chart showing which buttons are clicked
- Call, WhatsApp, Email, Directions
- Percentage of total clicks

**Top Locations**:
- Bar chart of visitor locations
- Countries and cities
- Visitor count per location

### Filtering Data

- **Date Range**: Last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, custom
- **Branch**: Filter by specific branch
- **Event Type**: Page views, clicks, scans, leads

### Exporting Data

1. Click "Export" button
2. Choose format:
   - CSV (for Excel/Sheets)
   - PDF (for reports)
3. Select data to export:
   - Analytics data
   - Leads data
4. Choose date range
5. Click "Download"

## Lead Management

### Viewing Leads

1. Click "Leads" in the sidebar
2. Select brand and branch
3. View all captured leads

### Lead Information

Each lead shows:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Message
- Source (which microsite)
- Date and time
- Status (New, Contacted, Converted)

### Managing Leads

**Mark as Contacted**:
1. Click on a lead
2. Click "Mark as Contacted"
3. Add notes (optional)

**Mark as Converted**:
1. Click on a lead
2. Click "Mark as Converted"
3. Add conversion details

**Export Leads**:
1. Click "Export" button
2. Choose date range
3. Download CSV file

### Lead Notifications

You receive notifications when new leads are captured:
- **Email**: Sent to branch email address
- **WhatsApp**: Sent to branch WhatsApp number (if configured)
- **In-App**: Notification bell in dashboard

### Lead Routing

Leads are automatically sent to:
- Branch email address
- Branch WhatsApp number
- Dashboard notification

Configure routing in Branch Settings.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Log In**:
- Check email and password
- Try "Forgot Password" link
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser

**Microsite Not Loading**:
- Check if branch is active
- Verify URL is correct
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection

**QR Code Not Working**:
- Verify QR code links to correct URL
- Test with different QR code scanner
- Regenerate QR code if needed

**Images Not Uploading**:
- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Check file format (PNG, JPG, SVG)
- Try compressing image
- Check internet connection

**Payment Failed**:
- Verify card details
- Check card has sufficient funds
- Try different payment method
- Contact your bank

## Tips and Best Practices

### Microsite Design

1. **Keep It Simple**: Don't overcrowd your microsite
2. **Use High-Quality Images**: Professional photos make a difference
3. **Clear Call-to-Actions**: Make it easy for visitors to contact you
4. **Mobile-First**: Most visitors will view on mobile
5. **Update Regularly**: Keep information current

### QR Code Usage

1. **Test Before Printing**: Always test QR codes before mass printing
2. **Size Matters**: Ensure QR code is large enough to scan
3. **Contrast**: Use high contrast colors for better scanning
4. **Placement**: Put QR codes where people can easily scan them
5. **Track Performance**: Monitor scan analytics

### Lead Generation

1. **Simple Forms**: Don't ask for too much information
2. **Clear Value**: Explain why someone should fill out the form
3. **Quick Response**: Respond to leads within 24 hours
4. **Follow Up**: Don't let leads go cold
5. **Track Conversions**: Monitor which sources generate best leads

### Analytics

1. **Regular Review**: Check analytics weekly
2. **Set Goals**: Define what success looks like
3. **Test Changes**: Try different content and track results
4. **Compare Periods**: Look at trends over time
5. **Act on Data**: Use insights to improve your microsite
