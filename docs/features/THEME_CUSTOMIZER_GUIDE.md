## Troubleshooting

**Theme not changing?**
- Make sure you clicked on a preset theme card
- Check that the theme card has a blue border (indicates it's selected)
- Look for the "âœ“ Theme applied!" message

**Want to go back to default?**
- Click the "Reset" button at the bottom
- Or click the "Default" preset theme

**Custom colors not saving?**
- Click "Save Custom Colors" button
- Your theme will appear in "Saved Themes" section
- You can apply it again anytime by clicking it

## How It Works

1. **Preset Themes:** Click to apply immediately

3. **Save:** Click "Save Custom Colors" to keep your custom theme
4. **Saved Themes:** Access your saved themes anytime
5. **Persistence:** All themes are saved in browser localStorage

## Color Variables

The theme customizer controls these CSS variables:
- `--color-primary` - Main brand color
- `--color-secondary` - Secondary brand color
- `--color-accent` - Accent/highlight color
- `--color-background` - Page background
- `--color-foreground` - Text color
- `--color-border` - Border color

These variables are applied globally across the entire dashboard.
