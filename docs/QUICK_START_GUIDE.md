This quick start guide will help you create your first digital business card microsite in just a few simple steps.

1. Go to **https://onetouchbizcard.in**
2. Click **"Sign Up"** button
3. Fill in:
   - Your name
   - Email address
   - Password (at least 8 characters)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email and click the verification link

## Step 2: Set Up Your Brand (2 minutes)

The onboarding wizard will guide you through setup:

### Brand Information

1. **Brand Name**: Enter your company name
   - Example: "Acme Corporation"

2. **Upload Logo**:
   - Click "Upload Logo"
   - Select your logo file (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
   - Recommended size: 500x500px

3. **Brand Colors**:
   - Click the color picker
   - Choose your primary brand color
   - Choose a secondary color (optional)

4. **Description**:
   - Write a brief description (1-2 sentences)
   - Example: "Leading provider of innovative solutions"

5. Click **"Next"**

âœ… **Done!** Your brand is created.

## Step 3: Create Your First Branch (1 minute)

### Branch Details

1. **Branch Name**: Enter location or division name
   - Example: "Downtown Office" or "Main Store"

2. **Contact Information**:
   - Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
   - Email: contact@example.com
   - WhatsApp: +1 (555) 123-4567 (optional)

3. **Address**:
   - Street: 123 Main Street
   - City: New York
   - State: NY
   - ZIP: 10001
   - Country: United States

4. **Business Hours**:
   - Set your operating hours
   - Mark closed days

5. Click **"Next"**

## What's Next?

### Customize Your Microsite

1. Go to **Dashboard** â†’ **Branches**
2. Click on your branch
3. Click **"Edit Microsite"**
4. Customize each section:
   - Hero: Add a welcome message
   - About: Tell your story
   - Services: List what you offer
   - Gallery: Add photos
   - Contact: Customize the form

### Generate Your QR Code

1. Go to your branch page
2. Click **"QR Codes"** tab
3. Click **"+ Generate QR Code"**
4. Choose size and format
5. Click **"Download"**
6. Print it on business cards, flyers, or posters!

### Share Your Microsite

Share your microsite URL:
- Add to email signature
- Post on social media
- Add to your website
- Print QR code on materials

### Track Your Results

1. Go to **Analytics** in the sidebar
2. View:
   - Page views
   - QR code scans
   - Button clicks
   - Leads captured
